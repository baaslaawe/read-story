Number words


Mr. Oliver says, “Mrs. Hill, do you have children?”


Mrs. Hill says, “Yes, I have three children.

I have one boy and two girls.

My boy is Ed.

My girls are Kim and Jill.”


Mrs. Hill says, “Mr. Oliver, do you have children?”


Mr. Oliver says, “Yes, I have four children.

I have one girl and three boys.

My girl is Pam.

My boys are Ned, Robert, and Will.”


Mr. Oliver says, “Mrs. Bird , do you have girls?”


Mrs. Bird says, “No, I have one boy.

My boy is Cal.”


Mrs. Bird says, “Uncle Ted, do you have children?”


Uncle Ted says, “No, I do not have children.

I have a pet shop.

I have five pups in my pet shop.

I have two snakes and four birds.”
