Thank you


Ann is a woman.

Bob is a boy.

Cal is a boy.

Dan is a boy.

Ed is a boy.

Fran is a girl.


Ann gives a bird to Bob.

She gives a cup to Cal.

She gives a dish to Dan.

She gives an egg to Ed.

She gives a fish to Fran.


Bob says, “Thank you for the bird.”

Cal says, “Thank you for the cup.”

Dan says, “Thank you for the dish.”

Ed says, “Thank you for the egg.”

Fran says, “Thank you for the fish.”


Fran gives an apple to Ann.

Ann says, “Thank you for the apple.”
