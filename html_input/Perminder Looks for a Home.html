Perminder Looks for a Home

Perminder needs to move. She wants a new home. Her apartment is too small. She wants a better neighborhood.

Her neighborhood is too noisy. Perminder wants to live in a quiet neighborhood.

Perminder is looking at a house today. The house has a big yard. It has three bedrooms, two bathrooms and a kitchen. Perminder loves the house because it has a beautiful kitchen and large bedrooms. Perminder also loves the neighborhood because there are many families.

Perminder wants to buy this house.
