The apple, the egg, and the olive


This is an apple.

This is an egg.

This is an olive.


The man picks up the apple.

He picks up the egg.

He picks up the olive.


The man has the apple in his hand.

He has the egg in his hand.

He has the olive in his hand.


The man gives the apple to the woman.

He gives the egg to the woman.

He gives the olive to the woman.


The woman has the apple.

She has the egg.

She has the olive.


The woman puts the apple in her dish.

She puts the egg in her dish.

She puts the olive in her dish.
